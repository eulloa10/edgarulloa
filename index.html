About

I am a California-based software engineer with a background in economics and accounting. Motivated by my passion for technology and automation, I made a deliberate career transition and pivoted into the field of software engineering.

I began the transition by taking computer science courses at a local college and developing Python programs to automate tedious accounting tasks. Eventually, I enrolled in an immersive full stack software engineering program to hone my skills and tie everything together.

While my expertise lies in developing apps using JavaScript and Python, I'm always eager to learn new languages and technologies to expand my skill set. I focus on building dynamic and efficient web apps that provide real value to end-users.

If you're looking for a full stack software engineer who can bring a diverse range of skills and experiences to your projects, I'm here to help.
